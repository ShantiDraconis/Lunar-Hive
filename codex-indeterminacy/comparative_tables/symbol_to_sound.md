**We do not know the sounds of Harappan symbols.**

Any symbol-to-sound mapping is:

## Methodological Constraints

For any proposed sound value:
1. **Comparative basis** required (related language data)
2. **Distributional evidence** (frequency, position patterns)
3. **Articulatory possibility** (see `phonemes/resonance.md`)
4. **Multiple alternatives** maintained
5. **Explicit low confidence**
