## Function Categories

### Phonetic
Represents sound, not meaning directly.
**Evidence:** High frequency, wide distribution, position-independent

### Logographic
Represents word or morpheme.
**Evidence:** Medium frequency, specific contexts, possibly pictographic

### Determinative
Semantic classifier, not pronounced.
**Evidence:** Position-specific, precedes/follows certain symbol classes

### Grammatical
Marks grammatical relationships (case, number, tense, etc.)
**Evidence:** Position-specific (often final), high frequency

### Numeric
Represents quantity.
**Evidence:** Repetition patterns, specific contexts (see `symbols/numeric_symbols.md`)
