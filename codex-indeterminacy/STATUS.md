- Multiple hypotheses are maintained simultaneously
- Confidence levels are explicitly marked
- Falsification criteria are documented

### For General Readers
- Nothing here is "decoded" or "solved"

- Alternative interpretations are always present
- Questions remain deliberately open

### For Contributors
- All additions must maintain epistemic humility
- Multiple alternatives must be documented
- Rejected paths must be explained, not deleted
- Confidence levels must be explicit

## Confidence Level System

When content includes hypotheses, they are marked with:

- **High Confidence** - Strong comparative evidence, multiple independent sources
- **Medium Confidence** - Reasonable evidence, some independent support
- **Low Confidence** - Speculative, requires more evidence
- **Experimental** - Exploratory, very preliminary

## Protection Statement

This status protects the repository academically and legally by:
- Not claiming definitive decipherment
- Maintaining epistemic boundaries
- Documenting uncertainty explicitly
- Allowing exploration without false authority

## Evolution

As hypotheses mature and stabilize, they may be:
